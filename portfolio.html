04 Case Studies

The work, in practice.

01
Public Sector Digital Contracting Policy
Public-sector legal digitisation
Coordinated with public institutions and policy stakeholders to support the rollout of a nationally recognised digital contracting framework for promise of sale and lease agreements.
The work contributed to the modernisation of a traditionally manual process and helped enable broader adoption of electronic contracting — a structural shift, not a technical patch. Navigating public procurement constraints, multi-stakeholder sign-off, and legacy documentation requirements, the result was a framework that held up legally and was actually adopted.

02
Change Management CRM Integration Automation
Change management & CRM-led contract automation
Supported a luxury travel client with a legacy workflow and low internal adoption of new tools — designed an automated contract journey aligned with the team's existing CRM-based way of working.
Connected the CLM system, e-signature platform, CRM, and email notifications through Zapier. The result: smoother transition, fewer manual touchpoints, faster turnaround times, and significantly improved user adoption. The key was meeting the team where they already were — not forcing them into a new system from scratch.

03
Workflow Automation Post-Signature Deadline Management
Post-signature workflow automation
Built an end-to-end post-signature automation replacing manual checks — once signed, CRM updated, welcome email sent, new CLM record created with signed document and key dates.
Also implemented a deadline reminder workflow alerting the responsible team member 30 days before contract expiry. Reduced delays, minimised human error, and improved scalability in a high-volume environment. The team went from reactive to proactive without hiring anyone new.
Read more ↓
04
HR & Onboarding Employment Contracts Automation
Employment contracts automation
Developed a spreadsheet-based automation for employment contract generation using predefined data fields, connected to a CLM through Zapier — replacing repetitive manual drafting entirely.
When a new row was completed with name, ID, salary, and email, the system generated the contract and sent it for signature automatically. Renewal reminders also wired in. The process made onboarding more consistent, faster, and less dependent on any one person knowing the workflow.

LegalTech Strategy LATAM Expansion Regulatory Mapping
Expansion to LATAM markets
Supported the adaptation of an AI-driven litigation platform across Latin America — aligning product design, legal logic, and regulatory requirements with local market realities.
Developed a comprehensive LATAM regulatory map, conducted UX and language testing in Spanish and Portuguese, and refined interface terminology and user flows.
